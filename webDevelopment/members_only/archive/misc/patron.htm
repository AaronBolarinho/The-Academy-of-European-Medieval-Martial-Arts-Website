is a founding partner, President and Chief Executive Officer of Taylor|Sprules Corporation, a firm which provides strategic communications services, marketing and branding to intelligent corporations. The firm has over 30 years of experience delivering complete, integrated solutions to a wide range of enlightened business leaders, senior government bodies and NGO's. He a also Director of the Royal Heraldry Society of Canada, Captain, and Captain of the Honourable Guard and Field Units of the 78th Fraser Highlanders, York Garrison. He has been responsible for developing complete integrated brand programs for over 50 national and international corporations.

Robbie has an intense interest in heraldry and its history and application, in such historical events such as jousts, tournaments and pas d'armes. His interest in this area, led to discussions with David when AEMMA was organizing their first pas d'armes hosted by the Royal Ontario Museum in 2008, to which he agreed to take part of the event as its Patron. Robbie donated the prize, a broadsword which he presented to the armoured combatant (the appellants) who was voted "first amongst equals", and presented the tournament medalion to each of the combatants at the conclusion of the event. He continues to donate prizes for the "first amongst equals", and presents the medalions at these events.
