Society Policies and Procedures Handbook

This Handbook has been developed to place as much Heraldry Society administrative material as possible in a single volume and in readily accessible form. In the past, a great deal of time has been wasted by Officers and Directors in an attempt to locate policies, terms of reference, motions and the like, made at previous times and buried in old minutes or correspondence. The present volume seeks to facilitate the process.

Since policies and administrative details change over time, nothing contained herein should be regarded as carved in stone. Most sections should be checked over annually to ensure they are up to date and amended as necessary. To avoid conflict between older and more recent versions, every amendment should be dated and prior versions discarded.

The distribution of the Handbook will normally be to the Administrator and Society Officers. It should, wherever possible, be by electronic means, as providing the easiest means of distribution and amendment. However, there is nothing either privileged or secret about the contents and they may be made available to any Director or Associate (or, in fact, any member) for whom they will be of value.

It is hoped the Handbook will prove of value to the Society in the future.

Kevin Greaves
2003
